## Ranking Methodology

Exercises are ranked by a scoring algorithm that considers:

1. **Core movements** - Big 5 compounds (Bench, Squat, Deadlift, Row, Press) get highest priority
2. **Common keywords** - Exercises with common terms (curl, extension, press) rank higher
3. **Equipment type** - Barbell > Dumbbell > Machine > Cable > Bodyweight
4. **Muscle groups** - Major muscle groups (chest, back, legs) rank higher
5. **Compound movements** - Multi-joint exercises rank higher
